ABOUT

Delhi Public School Ghaziabad Vasundhara a learning school was crystallized on May 3, 1999.

DPSGV is an established and progressive Senior Secondary School , a school with a unique history, strong roots and a developmental outlook which enhances the ability of the learner in body, mind and spirit.

The school provides a healthy platform which helps the students to scan the opportunities for growth, both at personal and social levels, a school that is known for its distinct culture, policies and practices, a school that represents a vibrancy and sensitivity to the needs of all stakeholders.

DPSGV is adaptive to challenges ahead, with flexible yet structured policies and a human perspective.

The school firmly believes in relevant learning through innovative teaching learning strategies which enhances the skills and prepares the child for the world and also believes that the quality of education is not to be measured by its length and breadth, but by its depth.
